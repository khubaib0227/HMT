About Us

Crafting excellence for over 50 years

Our Story

Hard Metal Tools was founded in 1973 with a simple mission: to provide professional-grade tools that professionals could trust. What began as a small tool manufacturing workshop has grown into one of the most respected names in the industry.

For over five decades, we've been committed to crafting tools that endure. Our founders believed that the right tool should last a lifetime, and that philosophy guides every decision we make today.

We've remained independent, family-owned, and dedicated to the principles that built our reputation: quality, reliability, and unwavering customer support.
